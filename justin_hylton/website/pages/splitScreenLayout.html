Questions

  1. Do you require deposits for appointments?
  2. Do you have a cell phone policy?
  3. How can I book an appointment?
  4. Should I shower before a treatment?
  5. What Time Should I Arrive?
  6. What Should I Wear?

Answers

  1. No deposits required for appointments.
  2. Although there isn't a phone policy in place, it's advised that phones be turned off or switched to mute for the optimal experience.
  3. You can schedule an appointment by calling or messaging us on WhatsApp.
  4. It is mandatory to shower prior to receiving treatment since it is a hygienic measure and helps to eliminate any lotions, oils, or perfumes that you could have used before the appointment.
  5. You should arrive at least 15-30 minutes before your appointment to have ample time to get accustomed to the spa's environment.
  6. Wear comfortable clothing like a swimsuit, robe, or loose attire, avoiding jewelry and heavy makeup, to ensure relaxation and ease during your spa experience.
