About Us

At Lando's Spa, we offer a comprehensive range of spa treatments tailored to meet the unique needs of each client. From soothing massages that melt away tension to invigorating facials that leave your skin radiant, our experienced therapists ensure that every visit is a journey of self-care and indulgence. Pamper yourself with our deluxe body treatments, including luxurious manicures and pedicures, designed to nourish and revitalize your hands and feet.


Since our inception in 2012, we have established ourselves as a premier destination for wellness and relaxation in Kingston. Our serene atmosphere, coupled with our dedication to excellence, has made us the preferred choice for discerning clients seeking a sanctuary away from the hustle and bustle of daily life. Conveniently located in Kingston, our spa provides a tranquil oasis where you can escape, unwind, and experience the ultimate in pampering.


At Lando's Spa, we believe in the transformative power of self-care and strive to create an environment where you can indulge your senses and rejuvenate your mind, body, and spirit. Whether you're seeking a quick escape from the stresses of the day or a comprehensive spa retreat, we invite you to experience the epitome of relaxation at Lando's Spa.
