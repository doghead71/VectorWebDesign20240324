About Russ Spa

Nestled in the heart of Kingston, Russ Spa offers a sanctuary where serenity meets relaxation. Whether you're seeking blissful pampering, therapeutic treatments, or a moment of escape, we invite you to embark on a journey of wellness with us. Russ Spa, every visit is an opportunity to unwind, renew, and discover the true meaning of relaxation. We look forward to welcoming you into our peaceful haven and guiding you on your path to tranquility.
