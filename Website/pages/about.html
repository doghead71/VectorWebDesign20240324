About Us

In the current dynamic business landscape, it is essential for office and facilities security to safeguard a firm from criminal activities while simultaneously establishing an environment that instills a sense of safety and friendliness among workers and visitors. Secure Co Ja has extensive expertise safeguarding the security of office and facilities clients across Jamaica. Our customized security services provide protection against physical crimes, as well as additional layers of security against cybercrime and fraud. If your current security partner does not provide this range of services, it is advisable to collaborate with Jamaica's rapidly expanding security firm.

Our Mission

Our goal is to transform the provision of security services in Jamaica by maintaining a commitment to practical, community-oriented, and adaptable services, while expanding their reach over the whole country. We possess the ability to promptly and effectively address any obstacles that may develop, using technology to ensure the safety and awareness of our customers. When our communities and businesses are more secure, we all benefit. .
